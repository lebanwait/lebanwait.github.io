A VR arcade is an entertainment venue where customers can experience virtual reality through high-end VR headsets and motion-tracking equipment. It allows players to immerse themselves in interactive games and experiences that feel incredibly realistic.

No experience is necessary! Our staff will assist you with setting up the equipment, understanding the controls, and selecting a game that matches your comfort level. Whether you’re a first-time player or a VR enthusiast, we ensure a fun and smooth experience.

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend making a reservation, especially during weekends and peak hours, to guarantee availability. You can book a session online, over the phone, or in person.

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and flat, closed-toe shoes for the best experience. Avoid bulky accessories, hats, or anything that could interfere with the VR headset and motion tracking.

Prices start at $10 for a 3-minute session.

We only accept payment via credit card and have the cash on-site option.
